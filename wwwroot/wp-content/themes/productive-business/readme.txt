=== Description ===

A free Multipurpose theme for WordPress.

Productive Business Theme for WordPress is a multi-purpose theme that has been designed ground up, with support for most website features. 
It is highly configurable, customisable and integrates easily with all popular WordPress plugins.
Some of the features to love about this theme include, ability to switch on and off header search form, 
ability to design your home page, with provision of generous and tactfully-placed Widgets on the homepage. 
It support 4 different layout, which you can choose for any page, posts on your website. 
It's easy and quick to install this theme, as is easy and quick tp customise and adapt to your taste.
